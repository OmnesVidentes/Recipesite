How to Make Corn Sasla

Corn salsa is fire. Its healthy, its cheap, and most importantly its easy to make

Here's What You'll need

  • Can of Organic Yellow Corn
  • Can of Organic Black Beans
  • Red Onion (Diced)
  • Roma Tomaoto (Seeded and Cubed)
  • Jalepeno (Diced)
  • Cilantro (Chopped)
  • Lime Juice (tbsp)
  • Vinegar (tsp) (opitonal)
  • Garlic

How to Prepare

  1. Dump prepared ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix it
  3. Enjoy it

Easy enough?
